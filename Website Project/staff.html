Our Team

Meet the team that makes the Krusty Krab the best restaurant in Bikini Bottom!

At the Krusty Krab, we're proud of our team of dedicated employees. We're always looking for new talent to join our team, so if you're interested in working at the Krusty Krab, please send us an email pressing the email icon on the top right or by pressing the rights in the bottom and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Eugene H. Krabs

Position: Founder and owner

Bio: Eugene H. Krabs, more commonly known as just Mr. Krabs, is the founder and owner of the Krusty Krab. He's a penny-pinching businessman who loves nothing more than making money, but he also cares deeply about his employees and the success of his restaurant.

Squidward Tentacles

Position: Cashier

Bio: Squidward Tentacles is the cashier at the Krusty Krab. He's a talented clarinet player, but he often finds his job tedious and longs for a more glamorous career in music. Despite his grumpy demeanor, he cares about the success of the restaurant.

SpongeBob SquarePants

Position: Fry cook

Bio: SpongeBob SquarePants is the fry cook at the Krusty Krab. He's an enthusiastic and optimistic sea sponge who takes great pride in his work and loves making Krabby Patties. He's also a loyal friend to all of his coworkers.

Patrick Star

Position: Secret employee

Bio: Patrick Star is a secret employee at the Krusty Krab. He's best friends with SpongeBob and often helps him out in the kitchen, but he doesn't technically work at the restaurant. He's not the sharpest tool in the shed, but he's always eager to help out his friends.

Training and Inspection

At the Krusty Krab, we take the training and inspection of our employees very seriously. All new hires go through an extensive training program that includes learning how to maintain personal hygiene, keep their work station clean and organized, and handle emergency situations.

To ensure that our employees are meeting our high standards, we conduct regular inspections. During these inspections, employees are evaluated on their ability to keep their work area clean, arrive at work on time, complete tasks assigned by their supervisor, and maintain personal hygiene.

We also keep annual report cards for each employee to track their progress and identify areas for improvement. Our goal is to provide our customers with the best possible experience and that starts with having a well-trained and dedicated staff.
